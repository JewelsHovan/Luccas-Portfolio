Welcome to My Portfolio

Hi, I'm Luccas Booth. I graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the University of New Hampshire and currently work as an art handler. My mission is to continually develop myself as an artist, to grow, and to design art in my own way. I believe in the authenticity of art and do not support its commercialization.

I have personally grown as an artist by traveling to many places and experiencing different cultures. Here is a short list of places I've been:

  • New York City, USA
  • Paris, France
  • Tokyo, Japan
  • Barcelona, Spain
  • Montreal, Canada

Each destination has inspired my work in unique ways!
